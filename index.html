Instructions on How to make shake

Just mix all of the ingredients in a blender then you have a shake!!!

Mango Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 Mango
  • 1 cup Ice
  • 1 tbsp Sugar

Chocolate shake

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup chocolate syrup
  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream

Strawberry Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup 2% milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1-1/2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 tbsp strawberry jam

Vanilla Shake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tspn vanilla extract

Oreo Milk Shke

Ingredients:

  • 6 pcs of Oreo
  • 3 scoops of vnill ice crem
  • 3/4 cup milk

Avocado Milk Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 3 tbsp honey

Banana Milk Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup milk

Cinamon Milk Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp white Sugar
  • 1 tspn vanilla extract
  • 1 tspn cinamon powder
  • 8 ice cubes

Watermelon Milk Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cup of watermelon
  • 2 1/4 cups of milk
  • 2 tspn white sugar

Lemon Milk Shake

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp lemon curd
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 tbsp lemon zest
